Selected Projects

Oct 2025 - Jul 2026 First Author · Oral Presentation

Layer Sensitivity Matters: Mixed-Precision PTQ for SAM2 Video Segmentation

Designed GLS-MPQ, a layer-sensitivity-guided mixed-precision post-training quantization method. Starting from a fully low-bit model, GLS-MPQ greedily restores sensitive layers to full precision under an average bit-width budget, prioritizing layers with the largest marginal improvement on PVS metrics.

I integrated the quantization interface into the SAM2 video predictor, built evaluation pipelines on DAVIS-2017 and SA-V, implemented mixed-precision search, and conducted ablations for M-KVM.

May 2025 - Oct 2025 National Champion · Challenge Cup

Starops: System-Level Agent for Intelligent Operations and Maintenance

Developed a system-level agent on the Kylin operating system for monitoring, anomaly detection, dependency analysis, automatic optimization, and system-level question answering.

The log anomaly module uses BERT for semantic encoding, LLaMA as a decoder, and a projector for aligning encoder and decoder semantic spaces.

May 2025 - May 2026 National Innovation Training Program

JARA: Joint Alignment and Reconstruction for Region-Aware Pre-training

Built a visual-context-reconstruction based image-text pre-training framework with ViT and BERT. I mainly designed Spatially Balanced Masking, which dynamically adjusts the mask sampling distribution to strengthen cross-region semantic modeling.
